===============================================================================
QUICKSTART FOR AI ASSISTANTS
===============================================================================

SETUP (one time):
  ./setup.sh

QUERY:
  ./query.sh "How many foxes at step 180?"
  ./query.sh "Generate a PDF report" --output report.pdf
  ./query.sh "50x50 grid, 200 steps" --validate

EXAMPLES:
  "How many rabbits at step 50?"
  "Show me foxes and rabbits at step 100"
  "What are the populations at timestep 200?"
  "Generate a report"
  "Create a PDF with default parameters"
  "Simulate with nx=30, ny=30 for 200 steps"
  "Validate against the baseline"

DEFAULT PARAMETERS:
  Grid: 50×50
  Steps: 360
  Seed: 12345 (for reproducibility)

OUTPUT FORMATS:
  --format text (default): Print to terminal
  --format pdf: Generate multi-page PDF with plots

See .instructions.md for detailed documentation.
See README.md for full guide.
